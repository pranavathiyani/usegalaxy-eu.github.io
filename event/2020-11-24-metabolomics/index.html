First Metabolomics Community Call

From 2020-12-10 to 2020-12-10 - Add to your Calendar

The first call of the Galaxy Metabolomics Community will be held on the 10th of December at 2 PM CET.

The bi-monthly community calls aim at bringing together Galaxy metabolomics researchers -including users and developers- to discuss about needs, ideas, scientific problems and to find new collaborators and synergies in the community. Everyone is welcome!

Subscribe to the mailing list and/or join the metabolomics GITTER channel to get the latest updates about this and other events.
