For attendance, please apply here\n\nThe Freiburg Galaxy Team and Deep-Sequencing unit at the MPI-IE Freiburg offer an introductory course to the analysis of high-throughput sequencing data using the Galaxy platform.\n\n

Important notes

  1. If you are registered but cannot attend our workshop, please contact usby email immediately. We have a long waitinglist and can give your place to others, even on short notice.
  2. Please register at https://usegalaxy.eu if you havenot done so already. For the MPI people: the European Galaxy server isdifferent from the Galaxy server of MPI: you will need an account on ourserver, too.
  3. You can bring your own notebook or computers will be available. Eduroam is available, ask your institute for how to login.
  4. The workshop is free of charge. Unfortunately no stipends for travel or accommodation are available.
