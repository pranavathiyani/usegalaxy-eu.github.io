Biocontainers at the nf-core Hackathon

training data

From 2020-07-17 to 2020-07-17 - Add to your Calendar

The nf-core hackathon in July this year will be fully virtual, so you can contribute to nf-core and get your pipeline started from the comfort of your homes. Bjoern will give a talk about Bioconda and BioContainers and how both technologies are building the fundament of todays reproducible science infrastructure.
