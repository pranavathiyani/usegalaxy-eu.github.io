Next Galaxy Workshop on HTS data analysis in September 2018

»
Jan 26, 2018
Freiburg, Germany
Freiburg training

Because many people already asked: The autumn Galaxy Workshop on high-throughput data analysis will take place from 17.-21.9.2018 here in Freiburg. Registration will be open from mid of April 2018 on. Save the date!

Supported by

The Freiburg Galaxy Team is headed by Björn Grüning and is part of the Bioinformatics Group Freiburg, headed by Prof. Dr. Rolf Backofen.
