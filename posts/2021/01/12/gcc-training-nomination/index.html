Nominate a training for the 2021 Galaxy Community Conference (GCC2021)

»
Jan 12, 2021
training GCC


The training topic nominations for the Galaxy Community Conference 2021 are now open.

If you have a training topic that you want to teach or learn, please fill out the form with your preferences! Once all the preferences from the community are gathered, there will be a period to vote for topics and decide together on the training programme.


Training topic nominations are now open for 2021 Galaxy Community Conference (GCC2021): https://t.co/Fc7WQKwdA2. Have a topic you want to learn more about, or present? Then please nominate it. https://t.co/As9vfTqdxF #UseGalaxy pic.twitter.com/1bkrjjBXJ6
