30,000 users

»
Apr 25, 2021
galaxy

The European Galaxy Server has surpassed the 30,000 registered users.

Since its opening in 2018, a total of 18 million jobs has been run and the number of registered users has grown from a few hundred to 30,000 nowadays:

The number of active users1 each month has also grown to an average of around 3,000 per month:

We would like to thank you all and our funders, the support from de.NBI, ELIXIR, EOSC-Life, the compute center, and the Faculty of Medicine at the University of Freiburg.

Special thanks also to our partner sites Erasmus MC, ELIXIR Belgium, the Institute Pasteur and the whole Galaxy community.

  1. Users that have run at least one job. ↩
