Sep 17, 2019
Freiburg devops data

This week we are celebrating already 11 million datasets, 6 million jobs run on UseGalaxy.EU and 8 thousand users. Wow!!

This would not be possible without the support of de.NBI, ELIXIR, EOSC and the Freiburg Faculty of Medicine.

Thanks to all our users and people that have contributed to this project! Special thankts our partner sites Erasmus MC, Belgium and Pasteur and the Galaxy community.
